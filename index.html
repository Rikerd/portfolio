About Me

My​ ​love for video​ ​games​ ​began when I first picked up a Game Boy at the age of four. ​​Video games allowed for me to live a life that was more than just being an average student in school. The intricate worlds and unique aesthetics allowed for me to be an adventurer or commander or whatever I desired within video games. These experiences are a huge part of my life and have been the driving force in my passion for software engineering and video game development as I hope to share these same experiences to many others.

My journey continues in UC Irvine, as my passion and dream led me to study Computer Game Science. During my years at the institution, I studied, designed, and engineered my own video games alongside my fellow peers and experienced many trials and success. This experience has helped me learn and strengthen both my skills in programming, as well as my ability to communicate and cooperate with my teammates as I strive to develop video games for many others to enjoy.

With this new knowledge and experience, my passion and dream for video games have not changed, and I am excited to learn even more about video game development within the industry.

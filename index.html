About Me

Hi my name is Richard But and I am a video game programmer and designer. In the past five years, I have been studying, designing, and engineering my own video games alongside my fellow peers and experienced many trials and success. This experience has helped me learn and strengthen both my skills in C# and Unity, as well as my ability to communicate and cooperate with others as I strive to develop video games for many others to enjoy.

My​ ​love for video games began when I first picked up a Game Boy at the age of four. Ever since then video games have always been a huge part of my life and has been the driving force in my passion for software engineering and video game development.

Recent games I have played and fallen in love with would have to include Persona 5 and Super Smash Bros. Ultimate. Other activities I enjoy in my free time would include watching anime and playing basketball.
